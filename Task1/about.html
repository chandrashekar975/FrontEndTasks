ABOUT US 
        The Cherabuddi Education Society was registered in January 1999 as an NRI-promoted society. The Society  aims at creating a 
        state-of-the-art engineering institution in association with leading NRI technology professionals and well-known academicians of the
        twin states of Telangana & Andhra Pradesh.  The Society aims to harness technical excellence with a commitment to the ethos of 
        'useful learning'. 

        Our students remain at the heart of our vision, benefiting from our innovative teaching methods, and close links with premier 
        companies and research institutions in India and abroad. Students will enjoy engineering and will have the chance to develop skills 
        that will last a lifetime.

        NRI Members

Dr. Raghava V Cherabuddi

Dr. Raghava is a BE (CSE), MS (Computer Science), MS (Computer Engineering) and PhD (Computer Engineering, USA). He was most recently with Microsoft Corporation, Hyderabad as an Academic Alliance Manager for nearly two years. Previously he was with Intel Corporation in the US for about 12 years. He has published over 10 papers in international conferences. After living in the USA for 16 years, he had relocated back to India to contribute to the technological advancement of the state. 

Dr. Noel N Deep

Dr. Noel Deep, MBBS, MD, FACP is a board certified Internal Medicine specialist in Antigo, Wisconsin, USA. He is the Chief of Staff and Member of the Board of Trustees of Langlade Hospital. He also serves on the American College of Physicians Board of Regents and is the current Chair of the ACP's national Council of Young Physicians. He is the recipient of several national and state awards including the American College of Physicians 2010 Walter McDonald Young Physician Leadership award and the 2009 American Medical Association's Young Physician Leadership award.
